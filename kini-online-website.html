FAQ

Got questions?

Do you offer payment plans? ▼

Yes! For projects over $300, we offer a 50/50 split — half upfront, half on delivery. Reach out and we'll figure out what works for you.

How long does a website take? ▼

Most websites are completed within 2–3 weeks from our kick-off call. Timeline depends on how quickly you can provide content and feedback.

What do I need to provide? ▼

Just your logo (if you have one), brand colors, content/photos, and any inspiration sites you love. We guide you through the rest on our kick-off call.

Can I upgrade from one-time to monthly? ▼

Absolutely. Many clients start with a website or strategy package and then roll into monthly support. We'll make the transition seamless.

Do I own my website? ▼

100%. We build on your Wix account. You own it forever — there's no lock-in with Kini Online.
